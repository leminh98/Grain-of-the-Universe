Outbreak Set (Mainly enemies that cause disease)

• Fleeting [spore]: 1Fi 1He 1Fe 1D. If Fleeting spore is defeated, either do 1 damage to an asset you control, or you become Infected.

• Exploding [spore]: 4Fi 3He 2Fe 1D1H. If Exploding spore is defeated, each investigator at this location test Fight (3) or Will (3). If the investigator fails the test, that investigator becomes Infected. If a skull token was revealed during the test, the test automatically fails.

• Hidden [spore]: 1Fi 2He 4Fe 1H. Aloof. Forced - At the end of the investigator round, if Hidden [spore]  is ready, each investigator at Hidden [spore] location becomes Infected.

• Outbreak Conglomerate: 4Fi 5He 3Fe 2D2H. Hunter - At the end of the investigator round, each investigators at Outbreak Conglomerate location becomes Infected. Investigators who are already Infected take 1 damage or 1 horror. Victory 1

• New Variants: If you are already Infected, this card gains Surge. Otherwise, test Will(4) or Fight(4). If you fail, you are Infected.
