Deadly sin set: Peril. Sin. Max limit two sin cards in your hand. Most of this set is hidden or promotes surge

• Envy: Sin. Peril. Keep this card in your hand. While this card is in your hand, if any investigators at your location during their turn play an asset or event, take 1 horror (to a maximum of 3 per round). Effect: when an investigator at your location play an asset or event, pay double the printed the card cost: Discard Envy. Flavor text: let me do one better. 

• Wrath: Sin. Peril. Keep this card in your hand. While this card is in your hand, your hand size is decreased by 5. Effect: At the end of your turn, deal 1 damage to each investigator at your location or take 1 damage and 1 horror: Discard Wrath. Flavor text: As long as something feels this anger. 

• Gluttony: Sin. Peril. Keep this card in your hand. While this card is in your hand, you can’t gain resource. Effect: After an investigator at your location gain resources, reveal Gluttony.  You gain that many resources instead. Discard Gluttony. Flavor text: If I’m not satisfied, no one else will be. 

• Sloth: Sin. Peril. Keep this card in your hand. While this card is in your hand, you can’t move out of your current location or take attack actions. Effect: At the end of the round, test Foot(3) or Fight(3). If you fail, take 1 damage. Discard Sloth. Flavor text: I would rather lie down and rest. 

• Greed: Sin. Peril. Keep this mythos card in your hand. While this card is in your hand, you can’t play assets or events from your hand (that costs resource). Effect: if you have gained three resources after this card is in your hand, discard Greed. Flavor text: money makes the world go round. 

• Lust: Sin. Peril. Keep this mythos card in your hand. While this card is in your hand, secretly named an investigator other than yourself. You can only commit cards to this investigator. Effect: If the named investigator succeeds two different tests, discard Lust. Flavor text: it hurts when your love is unrequited (unless you have pride)

• Pride: Sin. Peril. Keep this mythos card in your hand. While this card is in your hand, you cannot commit cards to other investigators. Effect: if you spend your turn at locations without other investigators, discard Pride. Flavor text: it’s lonely when you are on top

• 2 x Birds of a feather flock together: Peril. Swap a mythos card in your hand with a mythos card in another integrator’s hand. If no cards were swapped this way, this card gains Surge. 

• Deepest and darkest desire: Discard the top card of the encounter deck until you find a Sin card. Draw that card.
